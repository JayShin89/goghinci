   What is an NFT?

NFT stands for “non-fungible token” – one of a kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade. GOGHINCI treats NFT as a digital art and a membership.

   What is Ethereum?

   What is Metamask?

Metamask is a crypto wallet that stores your Ethereum. It’s needed to purchase and mint one of the collections of Goghinci. Having a wallet gives you an Ethereum address (i.e. 0xABCD….1234), which is also where your NFT is stored. Learn more about Metamask and how easy it is to use here.
